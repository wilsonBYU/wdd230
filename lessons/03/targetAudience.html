Target Audience

Our target audience is anyone from Guatemala, older than 18 years, with small to large local business that are trying to increase their contacts and want an exponencial grow. They are entrepreneurs looking for a way to increate their amount of clients and develop a solid contact network.

Personas

Ana Gonzales

Ana is 32 years old and is one of the local entrepreneurs, rised and born in Guatemala, she is looking for associates to increase her business. In the past 3 years she has developed a small cloting manufacturing company supported by local artisians. Her ideal is to increase the amount of sales and help the local woman that make the beautiful and traditional fabrics. She looks to expand the business locally and use the fabrics to create different products, from clothing to shoes, to ornaments.

Roberto Pedroza

Roberto is 28 years old and is owner of a small shoe factory in the city. In the last 2 year he has increase the yearly income to Q200,000.00, making it one of the. His purpose is to get new clients and provide his products to local users with special discounts for local people. His main goal is to increase the workforce and provide new job opportunities to local people at the same time that his business grow.
