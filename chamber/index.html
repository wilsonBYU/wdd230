Guatemala exports increase by 15%

Guatemala's exports visibly improved since the country's entry in the Free Trade Area of the Americas in 2004. Guatemala mainly exports coffee and clothes, each representing 12 percent of total exports. Other exports include: sugar, banana and precious metals. Guatemala's mains export partner is the United States (40 percent of exports). Others include: Mexico, El Salvador, Honduras and the Euro Area.
